*Data-driven pricing optimization system — competitor analysis, price elasticity, dynamic pricing, and revenue maximization.*

An intelligent pricing advisor that helps SaaS companies, e-commerce sellers, and service providers find the optimal price point for their offerings. Combines competitive intelligence, willingness-to-pay analysis, and pricing psychology to maximize both revenue and conversions.

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Competitor Price Intelligence | Monitors and analyzes competitor pricing across markets to identify positioning opportunities |
| Price Elasticity Modeling | Estimates demand sensitivity to price changes for informed pricing decisions |
| Dynamic Pricing Engine | Suggests real-time price adjustments based on demand, seasonality, and market conditions |
| Tiered Pricing Architect | Designs optimal pricing tiers and feature bundling strategies for SaaS and digital products |
| Revenue Simulator | Projects revenue impact of pricing changes before implementation |
| Pricing Psychology | Applies anchoring, decoy effects, and charm pricing techniques to boost conversions |

## Usage

```
analyze [product/market]   # Competitive pricing analysis
optimize [current pricing] # Suggest optimal price points
design tiers [product]     # Create pricing tier structure
simulate [price change]    # Project revenue impact
benchmark [industry]       # Industry pricing benchmarks
```
